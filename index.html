UTS Headline

By Jack Meyer

Cover illustration by Robert Hunter.

Edited by Gavrielle Jacobovitz, Sara Bell, and Julian Shen-Berro Photography by Elisabeth McLaughlin

On September 19th, 2019, the voices of Union Theological Seminary students echoed through the nave of James Memorial Chapel: “Woe to them who build their seminary by unrighteousness, their luxury condos by injustice.”

The students were paraphrasing the biblical prophet Jeremiah’s condemnation of Jehoiakim, a king who lived in reckless luxury through the oppression of those who labored. They weren’t criticizing a monarch, though, they were attempting to change the course of UTS history.
